VIA Rapid Green Line

This page was last updated on July 7, 2026.

VIA Rapid, or Advanced Rapid Transit (ART), is a Bus Rapid Transit system that is currently being planned and constructed in San Antonio. Set to begin operations in 2028, ART will (technically) be San Antonio's second Bus Rapid Transit system, as Prímo 100 and former Prímo 101 were considered to be BRT lines until the late 2010s.

The ART Green Line (North-South ART) will be a limited-stop rapid transit line that will operate primarily along San Pedro Avenue and South St. Mary's Street, replacing several local buses along those corridors. The corridor will feature dedicated bus lanes, tram-like bus stations, offboard fare collection, and upgraded traffic signals and pedestrian infrastructure.
