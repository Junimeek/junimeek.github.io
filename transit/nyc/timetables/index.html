The Metropolitan Transportation Authority's official timetables are a little strange. Oftentimes, it is difficult to scan the schedules for a specific departure. And for Queens in particular, the timetables no longer include a detailed map due to the recent Queens Network Redesign, making it difficult to find landmarks.

Below is a collection of timetables that I have personally created as a convenient alternative to the official timetables.
