Baldi's got a new schoolhouse, and more notebooks too!

It's Baldi's Basics, but with a simple twist: somehow, Baldi has raised enough money to expand the schoolhouse! And of course, more space means more classroom, which means more noteboos for your friend to forget. Like usual, find all the notebooks to win! It's THAT easy!

So, what's the deal with the Extended schoolhouse?

Fundamentally, this is very similar to the original Baldi's Basics in Education and Learning game (this means this is still a horror game!). The main difference with this mod is that there's brand new maps to explore, as well as a bunch of quality-of-life improvements over the original game.

  • Two new maps to play!
  • Recreations of (a few) feaures added to Baldi's Basics Plus, namely the Baldicator, the new voice clips, some SFX, some animations, and other small stuff
  • SIX new items: the Speedy Sneakers, the Attendance Slip, Diet BSODA, Crystal Flavored Zesty Bar, the Party Popper, and the Dangerous Hammer!
  • UNLIMITED MONEY!!11!1
  • A heads-up quick map
  • Small improvements to Baldi's AI
