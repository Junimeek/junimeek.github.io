Brooks / Medical Center Express


Status Route Description
  • Add new express route to operate during rush hours on weekdays only with a 30 minute frequency
    • Connects the South Texas Medical Center Transit Center and Brooks Transit Center via Interstate 10 and Interstate 37.
    • Stops at Crossroads Park & Ride, Centro Plaza, and Ellis Alley Park & Ride
    • Serves some stops along Babcock Road, Commerce / Market Street, and SE Military Drive
