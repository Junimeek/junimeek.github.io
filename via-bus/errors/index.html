VIA Pocket Schedule Errors

Some of the official VIA schedules have errors. The vast majority of them are minor visual errors and inconsistencies (most of which won't be listed here), but some errors convey inaccurate information. So, I have created this list of errors that you may want to be aware of below.

Route Route Name Error List ‡
24 East Houston
  • Wheatly Middle School is now the Young Men's Leadership Academy at Wheatley

  • River Walk RV Park is now the Mission City RV Park
    • 32 Steves
  • The bus does not travel on Elgin Avenue as the map suggests; it travels on the parallel Anita Street instead
    • 62 Kirk
  • Lowell Middle School is now the Kelly Academy
    • 76 West Commerce Skip
  • Wrenn Middle School no longer exists
    •

    ‡ Sometimes, the words outbound and inbound will be used in this table. Outbound and inbound refer to the order of timepoints in the official schedules. For example, Route 32 has timepoints labeled as A thru G, where the A→F direction is considered to be inbound, and the G→A direction is considered to be outbound.
