VIA Lineup Timetables

These schedules were last updated on July 18, 2025 to reflect the latest service changes on August 19, 2024.

Lineup is a service that VIA operates every night, with buses departing the Downtown Service Hub (near Travis Park) at 10:30pm, 11:30pm, and 12:30am to give riders a final chance to make transfers and reach their final destinations. VIA, however, does not provide dedicated timetables for lineup routes, instead scattering the times across their regular daytime timetables. This page contains custom timetables that I have made for routes 202â€“296 in the style of their daytime schedules as a convenient alternative to VIA's brochures.

When VIA announces service changes that affect lineup scheduling, new timetables will be uploaded here as fast as I can create them. The next service changes will go into effect on August 18, 2025.

Due to the upcoming introduction of the new Route 10 (Nacogdoches Skip) that is expected to run until 12:30am every day, be aware that every schedule will likely need to be updated to add it to the list of Downtown transfers.

Notes and Known Errors

These notes and errors apply to all three types of schedules (i.e., web, printable, and grayscale versions). Expect every error to be fixed within a few days of VIA releasing the schedules for the upcoming service changes.

  • [Note 1]: Although Route 48 IH-35 South Express ended service in 2020, the number continues to be referenced in Route 46's note about the lineup service.
  • [Note 2]: Lineup 282 is identical to daytime 82 (note that this contradicts what the lineup pamphlet says; 282 still operates on Grandstand Drive and Fairgrounds Parkway). Thus, the original schedule for route 82 is linked instead of a custom lineup timetable. I will eventually make a lineup variation for 282, but it is currently very low on my list of priorities.

  • [Error 202]: Point A on the Southbound side of the timetable should be labeled as "St. Mary's & Martin".
  • [Error 209]: The effective date is wrong; it should be 8-19-2024, not 8-19-2025.
  • [Error 225]: Route 209 is missing from the Downtown Service Hub transfer list.
  • [Error 230]: Immediately north of Point A, the bus is mistakenly portrated as turning left on Pecan Street. This should be corrected to a left turn on Martin Street instead.
  • [Error 276]: Point A on the Eastbound side of the timetable should be labeled as "St. Mary's & Martin".
