VIA Lineup Timetables

Lineup is a service that VIA operates every night, with buses departing the Downtown Service Hub (near Travis Park) at 10:30pm, 11:30pm, and 12:30pm to give riders a final chance to make transfers and reach their final destinations. VIA, however, does not provide dedicated timetables for lineup routes, instead scattering the times across their regular daytime timetables. Notes and Known Errors

These notes and errors apply to all three types of schedules (i.e., web, printable, and grayscale versions).

  • [Error 276]: Point A on the Eastbound side of the timetable should be labeled as "St. Mary's & Martin".
