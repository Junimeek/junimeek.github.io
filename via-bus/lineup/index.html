VIA Lineup Timetables

Lineup is a service that VIA operates every night, with buses departing the Downtown Service Hub (near Travis Park) at 10:30pm, 11:30pm, and 12:30pm to give riders a final chance to make transfers and reach their final destinations. VIA, however, does not provide dedicated timetables for lineup routes, instead scattering the times across their regular daytime timetables.
