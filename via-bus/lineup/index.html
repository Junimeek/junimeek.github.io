VIA Lineup Timetables

These schedules were last updated on August 16, 2025 to reflect the latest service changes on August 18, 2025

Updated schedule alert: If you downloaded the schedules for routes 209, 230, 277, or 289 before August 16, those schedules are now outdated. Please download the latest versions of these schedules down below.

Lineup is a service that VIA operates every night, with buses departing the Downtown Service Hub (near Travis Park) at 10:30pm, 11:30pm, and 12:30am to give riders a final chance to make transfers and reach their final destinations. VIA, however, does not provide dedicated timetables for lineup routes, instead scattering the times across their regular daytime timetables. When VIA announces service changes that affect lineup scheduling, new timetables will be uploaded here as fast as I can create them.

Printable Schedules

Would you like to have a physical copy of a particular schedule to bring with you while you're out and about? Using one of the download links below, you can do that very easily! Because the actual timetables are so short, all lineup schedules fit on a single piece of US letter paper (8.5 x 11 inches).

The printable copies of the schedules have lines outside of the actual content area. The solid lines are fold lines: just fold the paper along those lines. The broken (or dashed) lines are cut lines: after you fold the schedule, just take a pair of scissors and cut along those lines.

A quirk you'll notice about the printable schedules is that the second page (for the backside) is upside down. This is intentional by design; if you unfold and look at an official pocket schedule from VIA, you'll notice that they share the exact same quirk: the backside with the Saturday and Sunday schedules is upside down. This was done to ensure that the cover page is directly behind the map.
