VIA Lineup Timetables

These schedules were last updated on July 18, 2025 to reflect the latest service changes on August 19, 2024.

Lineup is a service that VIA operates every night, with buses departing the Downtown Service Hub (near Travis Park) at 10:30pm, 11:30pm, and 12:30am to give riders a final chance to make transfers and reach their final destinations. VIA, however, does not provide dedicated timetables for lineup routes, instead scattering the times across their regular daytime timetables. Due to the upcoming introduction of the new Route 10 (Nacogdoches Skip) that is expected to run until 12:30am every day, be aware that every schedule will likely need to be updated to add it to the list of Downtown transfers.
