Effective date 8-18-2025, final revisions

On January 12, 2026, Routes 209, 243, and 296 will be eliminated. 209 will be eliminated due to low ridership, and 243 and 296 will be eliminated in favor of extended service hours of their respective daytime routes. In addition, the service hours of Routes 20, 68, and 534 were extended.

These changes once again require every schedule to be updated to reflect the new service patterns.
