Upon updating lineup schedules, the older versions will still be available for viewing down below. Just click any of the arrows to expand each section.

For the most part, the schedules will be categorized by the dates they go into effect from newest to oldest.

Effective date 8-18-2025, initial release

The initial batch of schedules for the August 18, 2025 service changes were created around three weeks before they went into effect. I rely on Transit's schedule data to determine times that aren't listed on VIA's timetables, however that data was not available at the time of me creating the new schedules.

The scheduling data has since been made available, and four timetables needed to be corrected. The original versions of those corrected timetables are listed down below.
