SayakaUI

SayakaUI is the lightweight web engine that I use to run the majority of this website. It is entirely developed in-house, with no plans to officially release it for general usage since using it is not exactly the most user-friendly experience.

SayakaUI is named after the fictional character Sayaka Miki from the anime series Puella Magi Madoka Magica. I believe that Madoka Magica is the greatest show ever made, and I wanted to honor it when naming the engine. My favorite character is actually Kyoko Sakura, however, a lot of my motivation for creating and maintaining this site comes from one of my closest friends. His favorite character is Sayaka Miki; therefore, I went with SayakaUI to honor both the show and one of my best friends.
